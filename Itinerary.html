2018 NASA Trip

Our Goals

This trip was run successfully in 2014 and 2016 and was a wonderful experience for the students. We are offering a similar experience again in 2018 during the April holidays.
The goal of this trip is To immerse the students in Science, Engineering and IT by:
  • Learning about the challenges in the NASA space programmes.
  • View and hear about the history of the space programme.
  • Take part in a simulated space mission at the NASA space camp.
  • Learn about the technologies and science used to create the theme park experiences.
  • Visit Silicon Valley to view and hear about some of the modern IT industry
    • To Develop teamwork, leadership and decision making through:
  • The Cape Kennedy space camp, simulated mission.
  • The experience of travel and living together for 12 days.
  • The experience of using public transport for parts of the SF experience.
  • Learn about studying in America by Visiting a University campus in San Francisco and gaining a understanding of what life on campus is like.
  • We hope to be able to provide both a educational and a once in a lifetime enjoyable experience for our students.

    • Itinerary

    Day 1
  • Depart Auckland in the evening and fly via Houston to Orlando, - nearly 20hrs of travel!
  • Note Approximately 4 Hour stop in Houston.
  • The order of the following days may be shuffled to suit.
    • Days 2,3,4
  • Space camp - Kennedy Space Centre includes, a day at ATX for 'astronaut training'.
  • lunch with an astronaut, bus tour of the launch sites and time to explore both the Atlanta shuttle and the Apollo exhibition.
  • NASA has just opened the new training center.
  • On the last day we will transfer to Orlando in the evening.
    • Days 5 and 6
  • Theme Park Day 1 - Universal Studios with a double park pass to both Universal studios and islands of adventure.
  • Includes a behind the scenes tour in Islands of Adventure
    • Days 7
  • A quiet day at the hotel in the morning, an opportunity to have some pool time.and recover from 5 intense days.
  • The afternoon we will visit the Orlando Premium Outlet where we will have our meal and stay for some shopping.
    • Days 8 and 9
  • Final day in Orlando, fly to San Francisco in the morning, Visit Golden Gate bridge in afternoon/evening including a cycle over the bridge.
  • Tour to Silicon Valley visit to HP and Computer Museum. We will also try and visit Stanford University campus this day as well.
    • Day 10
  • Visit to California Academy of Sciences and its planetarium and a visit to Alcatraz.
    • Day 11
  • Visit a morning at the Exploratorium, described as MOTAT on steroids followed by a visit to the Union Square Mall. Fly out early evening for NZ.
    • Days 12 and 13
  • travelling and coming back to New Zealand with 12 days worth of amazing memories
